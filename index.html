About The Event

About The Event

SHB Fest and Charity Concert 2023 are focused on channeling talent and passion for non-academic programs such as sports and arts as well as a way to promote our school using the competitions that are held. This event is also a way to help or students evolve and sharpen their talents and intellect. Training both responsibility, discipline and cooperation with our own students to give them the proper skills for their future. It is also a way to form bonds with schools that come to attend.
